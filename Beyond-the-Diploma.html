Beyond the Diploma: Thriving in the Real World - A Seminar on Guiding Fresh Graduates in Making Informed Career Choices

SPEAKER

Engr. Charles Finny Plata

Lecturer at Batangas State University - TNEU
Senior Pastor of Jesus the Anointed One Church Mataas na Kahoy

Insights...

Attending the "Beyond the Diploma: Thriving in the Real World" session conducted by Engr. As a mechatronics student, Charles Finny Plata is a wonderful opportunity for me. This lecture aims to provide vital insights into the difficulty of making the transition from academia to the professional sector. I'm eager to learn how to make sound career decisions and manage the early phases of my career. Engr. Plata's breadth of knowledge and insight will be invaluable in assisting me in understanding the practical parts of the industry, such as market trends, job prospects, and necessary skills for success. This knowledge will not only prepare me for the challenges ahead, but will also enable me to thrive as a mechatronics expert in the real world.

Furthermore, I expect the conference to throw light on the crucial non-technical abilities and traits that are frequently overlooked but are critical for job success. Soft skills such as effective communication, teamwork, and adaptability are essential in every field, and I'm excited to learn how to build and demonstrate these abilities in the workplace. Engr. Plata's advice will surely motivate me to approach my job with confidence and a greater awareness of the complexities of the real world, enabling me to make educated decisions and launch on a successful journey as a mechatronics graduate.

This document represents the recognition I received for my attendance at the webinar.
